=== Cookie Attributes ===
setSessionCookie: session={id}; Path=/; HttpOnly; Secure; SameSite=Lax; Max-Age={seconds}[; Domain={cookieDomain}]
clearSessionCookie: session=; Path=/; HttpOnly; Secure; SameSite=Lax; Max-Age=0[; Domain={cookieDomain}]
Domain handling: cookieDomain === "" -> Domain omitted (localhost-safe)
